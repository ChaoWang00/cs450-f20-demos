We'll solve

$u''+1000(1+x^2)u=0$ on $(-1,1)$

with $u(-1)=3$ and $u(1)=-3$.

A_int = second_deriv+factor

Glue on the rows for the boundary conditions:

In [25]:
A = sps.vstack([
    sps.coo_matrix(([1], ([0],[0])), shape=(1, n)),
    A_int,
    sps.coo_matrix(([1], ([0],[n-1])), shape=(1, n)),
    ])
A = sps.csr_matrix(A)

if n < 10:
    print(A.todense())
