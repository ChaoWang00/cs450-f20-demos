Simple network¶

Let's define a simple "network": one input and one output $$ \vec{x} \rightarrow \vec{y} $$

One weight, $w$, such that $$ \vec{y} = w \cdot \vec{x} $$

Loss Function¶

Let's optimize the model $\vec{y} = w\cdot \vec{x}$ so that loss $$ w = \mathrm{argmin}_{w} \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n} \|t_i - y_i\|^2 $$

def dloss(w, x, t):
    return 2 * (1/len(x)) * np.sum((-x) * (t - w * x))
