Rosenbrock¶

Let's look at a Rosenbrock function $$ f(x,y) = (a-x)^2 + b(y-x^2)^2 $$ which has a minimum at $(x,y)=(a,a^2)$

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rosenbrock_function

In [12]:
def f(x,y):
    a = 1
    b = 100
    return (a - x)**2 + b*(y - x**2)**2
